# Design references

## 1. Dribbble - Healthcare & Medical Clinic Website

Source: https://dribbble.com/shots/27360354-Healthcare-Medical-Clinic-Website

What I am using from it:

- appointment-first service structure
- clear service categories
- trust-focused content hierarchy
- simple navigation built around user tasks
- restrained medical/service-business layout discipline

What I am not copying:

- the clinic subject matter
- exact colors
- exact layouts
- decorative healthcare imagery

## 2. Awwwards - Trafft Booking SaaS

Source: https://www.awwwards.com/sites/trafft-booking-saas

Saved local reference:

- `design-references/awwwards-trafft-booking-saas.jpg`

What I am using from it:

- booking as the main product action
- strong public-site rhythm
- clear conversion path
- clean service-industry positioning

What I am not copying:

- SaaS-specific messaging
- illustration style
- exact visual language

## Applied direction

The final prototype should feel structured like the SEG3525 portfolio, but not visually identical. The design uses clear sections, large confident typography, visible information hierarchy, thin structural lines, and restrained motion. The color direction is warmer and more service-oriented: ink, off-white, moss green, and safety orange.
